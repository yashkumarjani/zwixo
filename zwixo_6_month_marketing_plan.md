# ZWIXO: 6-Month Marketing Strategy & Daily Content Routine

This blueprint outlines a practical, daily 30-45 minute content creation workflow and a 6-month strategic roadmap for **ZWIXO Memory Studio** (launching June 25, 2026). It uses your current toolkit: **ChatGPT** (storyboarding, poster prompts, copy), **Gemini Video AI** (10-second video clips), and **CapCut** (stitching, trending audio).

---

## 1. ZWIXO Daily Marketing Routine (30-45 Mins/Day)

To grow your business consistently, follow this structured routine from **Monday to Saturday**:

| Time Block | Duration | Task | Tool Used |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Morning (Planning)** | 10 mins | Write a 10s video script / storyboard or a poster concept. | **ChatGPT** |
| **Afternoon (Creation)** | 20 mins | Generate the poster visual OR create the 10-second video clip. | **ChatGPT (DALL-E) / Gemini Video AI** |
| **Evening (Post-Processing)** | 10 mins | Add trending music/captions, stitch clips if needed, and publish. | **CapCut + Instagram/WhatsApp** |
| **Night (Engagement)** | 5 mins | Reply to comments, direct messages, and share on WhatsApp Status. | **WhatsApp Business** |

> [!TIP]
> **Batch Content on Sundays (60 Mins)**: Instead of creating daily, batch-create 3-4 posts/videos on Sunday. This keeps you ahead of schedule and reduces daily launch stress.

---

## 2. The 10-Second Video Formula

Since Gemini generates high-impact 10-second video clips, use this high-converting formula for short-form video ads:

* **0s - 3s (The Hook)**: Show the problem. (e.g., A torn, faded childhood photo; or a calendar ticking away to a wedding).
* **3s - 7s (The Solution)**: Show the magic reveal. (e.g., A hand sweeping over the photo revealing a clean HD restoration; or beautiful countdown posters sliding out).
* **7s - 10s (The CTA)**: Direct call to action. (e.g., "Order in 30 seconds over WhatsApp. Link in bio!").

### Phase 1: Pre-Launch & Launch Week (June 14 - June 30, 2026)
**Goal**: Build curiosity and capture early orders via a launch offer.
* **Content Themes**: 
  * "Memories are fading: How are you keeping them alive?"
  * ZWIXO website reveal / "Coming Soon" teaser.
  * Launch Offer: Flat 20% off on all services for the first 50 orders.
* **Daily Action Item**: Share behind-the-scenes mockups and count down the days (e.g., "3 Days to Go").

### Phase 2: Portfolio & Emotional Credibility (July 2026)
**Goal**: Build trust by showing your output.
* **Content Themes**: 
  * Before vs. After video showcases (Photo Restoration).
  * "Ordering from WhatsApp is simpler than ordering food" (Explain the 30s ordering process).
  * Emotional storytelling: "Restoring my grandfather's 1965 wedding photo."
* **Daily Action Item**: Post 3 before-and-after reels per week showing the detailed cracks fading away.

### Phase 3: Occasions, Festivals & Winter Weddings (August - September 2026)
**Goal**: Leverage Raksha Bandhan, Janmashtami, and winter wedding prep.
* **Content Themes**: 
  * "The ultimate gift for your sister: Restore her favorite childhood photo" (Raksha Bandhan).
  * "Winter weddings are coming: Order your 15-day WhatsApp countdown poster packs today."
  * Baby Milestone Card reveals (Cute designs).
* **Daily Action Item**: Run occasion-specific discounts and showcase custom gift packaging.

### Phase 4: User-Generated Content (UGC) & Referrals (October 2026)
**Goal**: Let your customers do the selling.
* **Content Themes**: 
  * WhatsApp chat screenshots showing happy customer reviews ("Mom cried seeing the photo!").
  * Unboxing reels showing printed milestone cards or restored frames.
  * Referral offer: "Gift a memory to a friend, get your next restoration free."
* **Daily Action Item**: Post customer screenshots to your stories and save them in a highlight named "Happy Clients".

### Phase 5: Local Partnerships & Scaling (November - December 2026)
**Goal**: Expand reach in Ahmedabad and scale up operations.
* **Content Themes**: 
  * Collaborations with local wedding planners and local baby photo studios.
  * Behind-the-scenes artistry (showing manual coloring or vector creation).
  * Gift guides for anniversaries and milestones.
* **Daily Action Item**: Reach out to 3 local micro-influencers per week to offer a free restoration in exchange for a review video.
